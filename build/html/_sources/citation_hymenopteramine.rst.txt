 Citation
 ========
    Please cite the use Hymenoptermine as follows:

Elsik, C., Tayal, A., Diesh, C., Unni, D., Emery, M., & Hagen, D. (2015, July 6). Hymenopteramine. Retrieved [Date], from http://genomes.missouri.edu:8080/hymenopteramine/begin.do

    Please cite the use of a specific page in Hymenoptermine as follows:

Elsik, C., Tayal, A., Diesh, C., Unni, D., Emery, M., & Hagen, D. (2015, July 6). [Title of page]. Retrieved [Date], from [link of page]

    Please cite the use of any other tools and data from the HGD website as follows:

Munoz-Torres MC, Reese JT, Childers CP, Bennett AK, Sundaram JP, Childs KL, Anzola JM, Milshina N, Elsik CG. 20011. Hymenoptera Genome Database: integrated community resources for insect species of the order Hymenoptera. Nucleic Acids Research 39: D658-D662. In addition, please cite the use of genome consortium data with the appropriate genome consortium publication.
